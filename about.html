About Us

Urban farming made easy

In 2014, Business Gladius Africa (BGA) was born from a need to see that the average business owner is positioned rightly for success in the large market. Over the years, Business Gladius Africa has worked as a manufacturer's representative and marketing service provider to boost numerous company products and services so that they can meet the ever-growing market demands.

Eleven years later, Business Gladius Africa has garnered success working with Small and Mid-sized Enterprises (SMEs) and has expanded efforts into empowering Nigerians in the agribusiness sector through a subsidiary company, Gladius Urban Farm Africa, aka GUDFAMA.

What is GUDFAMA?

GUDFAMA is a subsidiary of Business Gladius Africa (BGA), created to solve the food crisis in Nigeria and Africa caused by the explosive rural-urban migration.

OUR MISSION

To solve the food crisis in Nigeria and Africa caused by the explosive rural-urban migration.
