Services

Our Services

We provide opportunities for individuals to farm virtually and learn from anywhere worldwide. Through the GudFama Assistance Program, the GudFama Ajuju Program and the GudFama Community.

Assistance Program

Own from 500 fish, ROI in 4 months Individuals aged 25 and above can subscribe to this program in one or two instalments.

Ajuju Program

Daily, weekly or monthly contribution program where users can farm, learn and earn extra money at the end of 4 months.

Gudfama Community

Active community of over 100 handson and virtual farmers, consultants, experts and agribusiness investors.

GudFama Farm Boss Program

This program is open to Gudfama Upgraded subscribers who have completed a 4-month Farming Cycle with Gudfama. They have the opportunity to be trained and own their own fully equipped and funded farm.

About Us

GUDFAMA, a subsidiary of Business Gladius Africa (BGA), was established to address Nigeria and Africa's food crisis caused by rural-urban migration. Born in 2014, BGA has worked as a manufacturer's representative and marketing service provider, focusing on SMEs. In 2025, it expanded its efforts into the agribusiness sector with GUDFAMA.
